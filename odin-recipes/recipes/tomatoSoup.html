Creamy Tomato Basil Soup

Description

This comforting soup blends rich, fire-roasted tomatoes with heavy cream and aromatic fresh herbs. It pairs beautifully with a classic grilled cheese sandwich and works perfectly for batch cooking. Soup enthusiast Sarah shares, "It is thick, velvety, and takes less than thirty minutes to make."

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 large yellow onion, diced
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 (28 ounce) cans crushed tomatoes
  • ½ cup heavy whipping cream

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. Heat olive oil in a large stockpot over medium heat and sauté the onion and garlic until translucent.
  3. Pour in the crushed tomatoes and vegetable broth, bringing the mixture to a gentle simmer.
  4. Stir in the fresh chopped basil leaves and season with salt and black pepper.
  5. Remove from heat, blend with an immersion blender until smooth, and stir in the heavy cream.
