Classic Chocolate Chip Cookies

Description

This timeless cookie recipe delivers a perfectly soft, chewy center with delightfully crisp edges. Bakers enjoy customizing the dough by tossing in chopped walnuts, pecans, or swapping in dark chocolate chunks. Avid baker Mark notes, "This is the absolute standard for a rainy Sunday afternoon."

Ingredients

  • 1 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • ¾ cup granulated white sugar
  • ¾ cup packed brown sugar
  • 2 large eggs, room temperature
  • 2 cups semi-sweet chocolate chips

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. Cream the softened butter, white sugar, and brown sugar together in a large bowl until smooth.
  3. Beat in the eggs one at a time, then gently stir in the vanilla extract.
  4. Combine the flour, baking soda, and salt before slowly mixing into the wet ingredients.
  5. Fold in the chocolate chips by hand and drop rounded spoonfuls onto a baking sheet.
