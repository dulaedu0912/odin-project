Slow Cooker Lasagna

Description

This crockpot recipe features a savory beef and tomato sauce simmering together for robust flavor. Home cooks love tweaking the dish, swapping cheeses and adding sausage, to match their preference. Allrecipes member Cathy explains, "I will never do another Lasagna in the oven."

Ingredients

  • 1 pound lean ground beef
  • 1 medium onion, chopped
  • 2 teaspoons minced garlic
  • 1 (29 ounce) can tomato sauce
  • 1 (6 ounce) can tomato paste

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. Cook ground beef, onion, and garlic in a large skillet over medium heat until the meat is browned
  3. Add tomato sauce, tomato paste, salt, and oregano and stir until well combined and heated through.
  4. Stir mozzarella, cottage cheese, and Parmesan together in a large bowl.
  5. Spoon a layer of the meat mixture onto the bottom of a slow cooker.
